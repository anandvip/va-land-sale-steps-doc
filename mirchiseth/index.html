Naveen Firewall Sheet
Partition/Title first. Sale later. 100% white only. Keep conversations short ➜ pivot ➜ redirect.

Core Technique

Pivot: Short, non-specific answer that ends the topic.

Step 1

Redirect: Immediately ask about partition/title to shift focus.

Step 2

Repeat: Use the same pattern every time to train expectations.

Step 3

Non‑Negotiables

  • All‑white transaction only. No cash, no adjustments, no third‑party conversions.

  • Buyer identity, price, and advance are confidential until registry.

  • Naveen’s role (if any) is partition & title assistance only.
